Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Milk powder
  • All-purpose flour
  • Baking soda
  • Sugar
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Prepare sugar syrup by boiling sugar and water; keep it warm.
  2. Mix milk powder, flour, and baking soda to form a soft dough.
  3. Shape small balls and fry slowly in ghee until golden brown.
  4. Soak hot gulab jamuns in warm syrup and serve.

Rasmalai

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Lemon juice
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom
  • Saffron

Recipe

  1. Boil milk and curdle it using lemon juice to prepare chenna.
  2. Form flat discs and cook them in sugar syrup until soft.
  3. Prepare thickened flavored milk with saffron and cardamom.
  4. Soak chenna discs in the flavored milk and chill before serving.

Kala Jamun

Ingredients

  • Khoya
  • Paneer
  • All-purpose flour
  • Sugar
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Mix khoya, paneer, and flour to make a smooth dough.
  2. Shape into balls and deep fry on low heat until dark brown.
  3. Prepare sugar syrup flavored with cardamom.
  4. Soak fried jamuns in warm syrup for enhanced taste.
